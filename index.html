Nice to meet you!

Kevin Chu is an experienced Kinesiologist who works at Kinex Active Rehab. He is dedicated to helping those with mental and physical health barriers recover through exercise. Through smart selection of exercises that fit your needs, he hopes to challenge you at a pace you are comfortable with, so that you can move closer towards your health goals.
