Train with Chu.

A Kinesiologist who works and works out at the same place... the gym.

Kevin Chu is an experienced Kinesiologist who is dedicated to helping those with mental and physical health barriers recover through exercise. He believes that exercise is a powerful solution to help combat disease and promote overall life satisfaction. Through smart selection of exercises that fit your needs, he hopes to challenge you at a pace you are comfortable with, so that you can move closer towards your health goals.
